Who We Are

The Voice of Chandigarh's Furniture Industry

Chandigarh Furniture Association (CFA) was established in 2000 by a visionary group of furniture dealers with a bold ambition: to transform Chandigarh into a single, unified furniture market — the largest in India, and eventually, Asia.

CFA came into existence to empower and infuse new confidence in manufacturers, dealers, and importers. Our objective has always been clear — deliver the best to customers while protecting and growing the interests of our members.

Today, with over 104 active member companies, CFA represents every segment of the furniture supply chain — from steel fabricators and mattress makers to hardware suppliers and interior decorators — all operating from Chandigarh's thriving Industrial Area.

Purpose & Direction

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To bring all furniture manufacturers, traders, and importers of Chandigarh together on a single platform — providing structured business development, infrastructure growth, security, and representation in multiple forums across PAN India and beyond.

We believe progress is made faster when we unite around common requirements, speak with one voice, and support each other's growth.

Our Vision

To become the largest and most respected furniture market association not just in India, but across Asia — growing our member base manifold and establishing Chandigarh as the undisputed furniture capital of the subcontinent.

We welcome all companies in related fields to join hands and become part of this prestigious, industry-defining association.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

Unity

Together we achieve what no single member can alone. CFA is the force that unites Chandigarh's furniture industry.

Integrity

Our association is built on trust, transparency, and fair representation for every member — big or small.

Growth

Driving business development, infrastructure expansion, and market access for all our member companies.

Community

CFA leads by example in social responsibility — blood camps, charitable work, and community upliftment.
