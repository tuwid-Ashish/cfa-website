Who We Are

A unified platform for furniture manufacturers, traders, and importers in Chandigarh's thriving Industrial Area — representing one of Asia's largest furniture markets.

Our Mission

To empower members with structured business development, infrastructure support, and a collective voice in policy and market matters across PAN India and beyond.

Our Vision

To grow Chandigarh's furniture market into the largest in Asia — increasing our member base, strengthening the supply chain, and delivering world-class quality.
