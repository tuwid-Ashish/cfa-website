About FAP

One Platform.
Endless Possibilities.

Furniture Association Punjab (FAP) is the leading trade body representing furniture manufacturers, traders, importers, and retailers across Punjab. We provide a unified platform to voice industry concerns, network with peers, and drive collective growth — locally and globally.

Our Mission

To unite and empower Punjab's furniture industry through advocacy, networking, and skill development — placing "Made in Punjab" on the global map.

Our Vision

To make Punjab a globally recognised furniture hub, known for quality craftsmanship, innovative design, and ethical trade practices.

Our Commitment

Solving policy challenges, facilitating business growth, and ensuring every member has the support they need to thrive in a competitive market.

Membership Benefits

Why Join FAP?

Joining FAP gives your business a powerful voice and a strong network to grow, compete, and succeed in Punjab's dynamic furniture market.

Industry Networking

Connect with 200+ manufacturers, traders, and suppliers across Punjab. Build lasting business relationships through regular chapter meetings and events.

Policy Advocacy

We represent your interests before government bodies — fighting for fair GST policies, ease of doing business, and better infrastructure for the trade.

Trade Exhibitions

Get priority access and preferential rates at trade shows and exhibitions — showcasing your products to buyers across India and globally.

Market Intelligence

Access exclusive industry data, market trends, and reports that help you make informed business decisions and stay ahead of competition.

Skill & Training

Participate in workshops, seminars, and training sessions on craftsmanship, design trends, business management, and export procedures.

Global Exposure

FAP actively works to take Punjab's furniture to international markets — connecting members with export opportunities and foreign buyers.
